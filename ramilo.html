YOUTUBE

ABOUT

YouTube is an American social media and online video sharing platform owned by Google.
YouTube was founded on February 14, 2005, by Steve Chen, Chad Hurley, and Jawed Karin,
three former employees of PayPal. Headquartered in San Bruno, California, it is the second-most-visited
website in the world, after Google Search. In January 2024, YouTube had more than 2.7 billion
monthly active users, who collectively watched more than one billion hours of videos every day.
